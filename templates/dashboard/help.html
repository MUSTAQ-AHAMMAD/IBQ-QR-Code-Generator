Quick Help Topics

Welcome to {{ app_name }}! Here's how to get started:

  1. Navigate to Generate QR Code from the sidebar
  2. Fill in your business card information
  3. Customize the appearance (optional)
  4. Click Generate QR Code
  5. Download your QR code and use it on your materials

All your QR codes are stored in My QR Codes. From there you can:

  • View all your generated QR codes
  • Search and filter by category
  • Edit QR code information
  • Download QR codes in different formats
  • Delete unwanted QR codes

Templates help you save time by storing your favorite QR code settings:

  • Create templates with your preferred colors and settings
  • Select a template when generating new QR codes
  • Share templates by making them public
  • Use default templates provided by the system
