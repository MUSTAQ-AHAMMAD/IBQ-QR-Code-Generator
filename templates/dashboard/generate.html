Business Card QR Codes

Your QR code will be generated in vCard format, which can be easily scanned by smartphones to save contact information.

Error Correction
  • Low (7%): Smallest size, minimal damage tolerance
  • Medium (15%): Balanced option
  • Quartile (25%): Good for print
  • High (30%): Best for logos, highest damage tolerance
Best Practices
  • Use high error correction for codes with logos
  • Ensure good contrast between colors
  • Test your QR code after generation
  • Keep the design simple for better scanning
